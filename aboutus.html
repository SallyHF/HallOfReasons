The Hall of Reasons Story

A virtual reasons haven created by a morbid fictitious team of unique individuals. It stemmed from a thought after the mastermind started her tech journey. She came up with 101 crazy reasons why she wasn't the so called stereotype to make it in tech. Before each lesson of her first CFG course, she would try to convince herself with reasons so out of the world and pessimistic on why that she shouldn't attend class.

She was glad she ignored those voices of so called logical reason in her head and pulled through.

Here she is now, trying to save a lost tech newbie, one at a time, one reason at a time in a most light-hearted manner.
